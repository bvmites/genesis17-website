Measure it

Round 1
In this round the participants will be shown 30 different images of various mechanical equipments. The participants need to identify the name of the component correctly. Each picture will be kept in display for 15-20 seconds and then will follow the next one. Once the display is over all the pictures need to be reviewed in a short span of 2 mins. Then the participants would be given 10 mins to complete any answers left. 8 teams or 16 participants will move to the Final round.

Round 2
In this round, each team will be given a sample part to check for dimensions. They will be given different instruments like Standard calipers, Vernier Calipers, Micrometer Screw gauge, Vernier Micrometer, Vernier height gauge, etc. (No dial or digital instruments are to be provided). Participants will have to determine various quantities like outer diameter, inner diameter, thickness, mean diameter, etc.The closest and most accurate answers are to be considered.

No. of participants per team: 2
Entry fees: 20
