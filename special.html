Special Event

Round 1
Teams from ET, EL, EE, CP, IT would be solving one puzzle and teams from PE, ME, CE would be solving a different puzzle. Two teams, who finish the puzzle in the least time, from each branch would qualify for the next round.

Round 2
A technical treasure hunt would be conducted and one team from each branch would move forward to the final round.

Round 3
This round would be a technical and non-technical combined treasure hunt. This treasure hunt cannot be completed without the help of fellow competitors. The team who finishes first would win the entire competition.
